Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 15, 2025

1. Information We Collect

  • Name, email address, and optional birth date
  • Medication data and reminder preferences
  • Device info like IP, browser type, and usage patterns

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To personalize your experience and provide reminders
  • To analyze feature usage and improve the app
  • To show non-personalized ads in the free version of the app

3. Email Communications

We may send medication reminders or system alerts. You can manage communication preferences in your profile.

4. Ads and Monetization

We display non-personalized advertisements through third-party networks such as Google AdSense. These ads do not use sensitive personal health information for targeting. You can opt to remove ads at any time by upgrading to a paid plan.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. Data may be shared with service providers only for essential app functions and with your consent or where required by law.

6. HIPAA Alignment

While Dose Ninja is not a Covered Entity under HIPAA, we align with HIPAA best practices, including:

  • End-to-end encryption of sensitive data
  • Access controls and secure authentication
  • Limiting third-party integrations to non-PHI systems

7. Your Data Rights

  • You may request, export, or delete your data at any time
  • Contact us at privacy@doseninja.com

8. Data Retention

We delete your data within 30 days of confirmed account closure or deletion request.

9. Children's Privacy

Dose Ninja is intended for users 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
